More

Rotaract Club is a service organization for young adults (18-30) sponsored by Rotary International. It focuses on community service, leadership development, and international understanding. Members engage in volunteering, fundraising, and networking to make a positive impact on society. Rotaract clubs offer young people the opportunity to connect with others in their community, develop leadership skills, and participate in various service projects and events. These clubs can be found worldwide and are often affiliated with local Rotary clubs.
